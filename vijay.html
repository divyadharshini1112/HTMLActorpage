Divya

The name **Divya** is of Sanskrit origin and means "divine," "heavenly," or "brilliant." It carries connotations of purity, beauty, and spiritual illumination. Often used as a feminine name in India and among Indian communities worldwide, it reflects qualities like radiance, grace, and a connection to something transcendent or extraordinary. The name is associated with positivity and light, evoking a sense of elegance and inner strength.
